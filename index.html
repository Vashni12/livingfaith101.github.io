GOAL OF BIBLE STUDY


Our hope for those who attend our Bible study is that they would gain greater growth in faith to have the heart of a disciple of Jesus Christ.


PROGRAM COVERAGE


The systematic Bible studies will cover the book of Genesis, the book of Exodus, the four Gospel Books, the book of Romans, the book of Galatians, the book of Joshua, and the rest of the Bible.


ONSITE & ONLINE


We provide onsite Bible study in Nashville, Tennessee, and we can connect with you through online video call services. Our goal is to deliver the Word of God to as many people as possible in the Tennessee area.

What is Koinonia?

The early Christians "continuously devoted themselves to fellowship." (Acts 2:42) The word for "fellowship" in Greek is koinonia, which means "to have in common" or "to share." As those who are united with Christ, we are to share the life of Christ with one another in a way that results in individual and corporate spiritual growth.

What we do.

In the same way, we gather together with one heart and mind to share our faith through different events. We do this expecting that the heart of love that Jesus showed us when he washed the disciples’ feet is received and shared in our Praise & Prayer Fellowship, Sports Fellowship, Guitar & Piano Class, Korean Language lessons etc.

FAITH STATEMENT

We believe…

  • in the Holy Scriptures as originally given by God, divinely inspired, infallible, entirely trustworthy; and the supreme authority in all matters of faith and conduct…
  • One God, eternally existent in three persons, Father, Son, and Holy Spirit…
  • Our Lord Jesus Christ, God manifest in the flesh, His virgin birth, His sinless human life, His divine miracles, His vicarious and atoning death, His bodily resurrection, His ascension, His mediatorial work, and His Personal return in power and glory…
  • The Salvation of lost and sinful man through the shed blood of the Lord Jesus Christ by faith apart from works, and regeneration by the Holy Spirit…
  • The Holy Spirit, by whose indwelling the believer is enabled to live a holy life, to witness and work for the Lord Jesus Christ…
  • The Unity of the Spirit of all true believers, the Church, the Body of Christ…
  • The Resurrection of both the saved and the lost; they that are saved unto the resurrection of life, they that are lost unto the resurrection of damnation.
